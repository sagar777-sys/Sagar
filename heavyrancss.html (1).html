Awareness About Heavy Rainfall

Heavy rainfall can lead to dangerous flooding and landslides, especially in urban areas where drainage systems can get overwhelmed. It's important to stay informed about local weather conditions and be prepared for potential evacuation orders.

SOME IMPORTANT POINTS TO STAY SAFE

1. Avoid Travel: If possible, stay indoors and avoid traveling in heavy rain, as roads could become flooded and dangerous.
2. Secure Outdoor Items: Make sure outdoor furniture, motorcycles, or any other items are secured to prevent them from being damaged or blown away by strong winds that may accompany the rain.
